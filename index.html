Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. researcher at Banaras Hindu University working in the group of Prof. Devendra Kumar Mishra. My work focuses on quantum optics, nonlinear and interferometric metrology using nonclassical light. I've collaborated with international labs including those at the University of Hamburg and Max Planck Institute. Broadly, I am interested in quantum metrology, two-photon absorption, and the design of quantum-enhanced optical setups.

Research Interests

  • Quantum interferometry and metrology
  • Non-degenerate two-photon absorption
  • Nonlinear quantum optics
  • Squeezed light, Schrödinger-cat states
  • Quantum information and sensing
