About Me

I am a Ph. D. student at Banaras Hindu University specializing in quantum optics, quantum metrology, and nonlinear optics. My research focuses on enhancing the sensitivity of interferometric measurements using nonclassical lights. I have collaborated with institutions including the Max Planck Institute and the University of Hamburg.

Research Interests

  • Quantum Optics
  • Quantum Information Theory
  • Nonlinear Optics
  • Quantum Metrology
  • Two-Photon Absorption
